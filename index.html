MISSION

At our society, we are committed to advancing the frontiers of biotechnology to foster a sustainable and harmonious coexistence with the environment. Our blue-coloured ring proudly represents our dedication to environmental biotechnology, where we strive to develop innovative solutions for the challenges facing our planet. With a deep grey motto guiding our path, we embrace the diverse realm of industrial biotechnology, seeking to drive progress and create meaningful impact across industries

VISION

About MOBBAS

MOLECULAR BIOLOGY AND BIOTECHNOLOGY ALUMNI SOCIETY

The Molecular Biology & Biotechnology Alumni Society is an association of all graduates of the Molecular Biology and Biotechnology (MBB) program at the University of Cape Coast, Ghana, recognizing the importance of fostering a strong alumni community and promoting MBB and pushing its members up the ladder. MBBists are basically in various groups:

  • Industrial Biotechnology
  • Medical Biotechnology
  • Agricultural Biotechnology
  • Bioinformatics
